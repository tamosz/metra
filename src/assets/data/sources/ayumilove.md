# Damage Formulas

> Source: [AyumiLove's Formula Compilation](https://ayumilovemaple.wordpress.com/2009/09/06/maplestory-formula-compilation/)

## General Formula
- **MAX** = (Primary Stat + Secondary Stat) × Weapon Attack / 100
- **MIN** = (Primary Stat × 0.9 × Skill Mastery + Secondary Stat) × Weapon Attack / 100

## Weapon Stats and Multipliers

### One-Handed Weapons
| Weapon Type | Primary | Secondary |
|-------------|---------|-----------|
| Sword | STR × 4.0 | DEX |
| Axe/BW/Wand/Staff (Swinging) | STR × 4.4 | DEX |
| Axe/BW/Wand/Staff (Stabbing) | STR × 3.2 | DEX |
| Dagger (Non-Thieves) | STR × 4.0 | DEX |
| Dagger/Stars (Thieves) | LUK × 3.6 | STR + DEX |
